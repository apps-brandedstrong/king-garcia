Based on Ceramic Pro's technology and its line of window films (KAVACA), here are the key technical specifications to differentiate these solar control and protection solutions:

1. Ceramic Tint (KAVACA Ceramic IR)
This is the high-performance window film line that uses ceramic nanotechnology to filter heat without affecting communication signals.

Nanoceramic Technology: It uses non-conductive, non-metallic ceramic particles. This ensures that it does not interfere with GPS, satellite radio, Bluetooth, or mobile phone signals.

Heat Rejection (IR): Its main strength is blocking infrared (IR) energy. High-end versions (such as KAVACA Ultimate IR) can block up to 99% of infrared rays, drastically reducing the interior temperature of the vehicle.

UV Protection: It blocks 99.9% of ultraviolet (UV) rays, which is equivalent to an SPF 1000+ sunscreen. It protects both the passengers' skin and the interior materials (preventing the dashboard and leather from cracking).

Optical Clarity: Unlike budget tints, it doesn't have a "foggy" or bluish appearance; it offers sharp vision even in low-light conditions.

2. Carbon X (KAVACA Carbon X)
This is a premium option that sits between traditional tint films and ceramic films, offering a balance between aesthetics and performance.

Carbon Infusion: It uses carbon particles to absorb heat instead of simply reflecting it.

"True Black" Aesthetics: Unlike other films that turn purple or brown over time, Carbon X guarantees a deep, stable black color that won't fade over the years.

Thermal Performance: It offers superior heat rejection compared to conventional tint films (tinted films), although it is slightly less efficient than the Ceramic IR line at blocking extreme infrared radiation.

No Interference: Like ceramics, since it contains no metals, it is "Signal Friendly".

3. Ceramic Pro Coating (The Liquid Coating) To differentiate it from films, this is the original liquid coating that cures onto surfaces. Nanomolecular Bonding: It is not a layer that "sticks" on, but rather chemically bonds to the pores of the surface (paint, glass, metal, or plastic), becoming a rigid and permanent protective layer. Self-Cleaning Effect: It creates a surface with such low surface tension that water beads up (extreme hydrophobicity), carrying away dirt and dust. Multi-Layer Resistance: It is the only system that allows the application of multiple layers to increase the thickness of the protection (as in the Gold Package), increasing resistance against fine scratches. Application Versatility: Unlike dyes or PPF that are only used on smooth surfaces, the Coating can protect the engine, brake calipers, interior textiles, and even rims.

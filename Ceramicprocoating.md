Here you have valuable technical and commercial information for each of Ceramic Pro's services, structured so you can use it directly in the product pages of your website.

1. Ceramic Pro Gold Package (The Gold Standard)
This is the maximum protection package, designed for those seeking a definitive solution.

Technical Configuration: 4 coats of Ceramic Pro 9H (the flagship product) + 1 extra coat on exposed areas + 1 coat of Top Coat (gloss and hydrophobic finish).

Surfaces Included: Full paint, wheels (outer faces), brake calipers, and windshield.

Valuable Fact: It is a permanent protection. It is not washed off and is reported on the Carfax history, increasing the vehicle's resale value.

Warranty: Lifetime Warranty.

2. Ceramic Pro Silver Package (Most Popular)
Ideal for owners who plan to keep their car for a considerable time (such as a medium-term lease or purchase).

Technical Configuration: 1 coat of Ceramic Pro 9H + 1 coat of Top Coat.

Performance: Offers the same 9H hardness as the Gold Package, but with less layer thickness, making it more affordable while maintaining professional protection.

Warranty: 5 years.

3. Ceramic Pro Bronze Package (Entrance Protection)
Designed as a superior alternative to any synthetic wax or sealant on the market.

Technical Configuration: 1 coat of Ceramic Pro Top Coat (or in some regions, one coat of CP Light).

Properties: Although it doesn't have the hardness of 9H, it offers extreme shine and impressive hydrophobic properties (high water contact angle), making washing easier.

Warranty: 2 years.

4. Ceramic Pro Sport Package (Fast Maintenance and Shine) This is a "refresher" service or an economical introduction to nanotechnology. Technical Configuration: Application of Ceramic Pro Sport. Technical Use: Widely used to maintain vehicles that already have 9H or as a standalone protectant that lasts approximately 6 months. It is excellent at repelling water spots and providing a smooth feel. 5. Ceramic Pro Wheel & Caliper (Wheels and Calipers) Specifically formulated to withstand the most extreme friction and heat conditions. Technical Specification: Withstands temperatures up to 750°C (1382°F). Added Value: Brake dust (ferrode) is highly corrosive and becomes embedded in the wheel clear coat. This coating creates such a smooth surface that brake dust is easily removed with pressurized water, without the need for harsh acidic chemicals. 6. Ceramic Pro Marine (Nautical Protection) A multi-layer system designed for the harshest environment: the ocean. Special Chemistry: Unlike automotive coatings, this one is designed to adhere to gelcoat and marine metals. Technical Function (Fouling Release): Reduces surface tension, preventing algae and barnacles from attaching to the hull. This improves hydrodynamics, resulting in fuel savings and increased speed. Protection: Prevents metal oxidation and gelcoat yellowing from salt spray. 7. Color Shift (Color Change PPF) This is a hybrid product that combines aesthetics and extreme protection. Technology: It is neither a paint nor a regular vinyl wrap; It is a Paint Protection Film (PPF - KAVACA) with integrated pigments. Technical Data: Unlike a traditional thin vinyl wrap, Color Shift has a thickness that protects against rock chips and has self-healing properties (scratches disappear with the heat of the sun). Aesthetics: It changes color depending on the angle of the light, offering a premium finish that conventional vinyl cannot match in color depth. Summary for your Website (Technical Keywords): 9H Hardness: Micro-scratch resistance tested by laboratories (SGS). Hydrophobic Effect: Water contact angle >110° so water rolls off and cleans the surface. UV Protection: Integrated sunblockers to prevent oxidation. Chemical Resistance: Tolerance to pH between 2 and 12 (strong acids and alkalis).

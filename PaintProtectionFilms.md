Here you have the technical information and coverage details for KAVACA Ceramic Pro Paint Protection Film (PPF) services, ideal for structuring the service sections on your website.

1. Ultimate Armor (The Ultimate Package)
It's the only package in the world that offers a lifetime warranty documented by Carfax.

Components: Combines PPF on the front with ceramic coating on the rest of the vehicle.

Coverage:

Full Front PPF: Hood, bumpers, front fenders, mirrors, and headlights.

Ceramic Pro Coating: Application to all remaining painted surfaces, wheels, glass, and plastics.

Technical Value: It's a hybrid protection system; the PPF protects against physical impacts (stones), and the ceramic coating protects against chemicals and weather.

2. Gloss Pro Paint Protection Film (Gloss Finish)
KAVACA's flagship line, known for its optical clarity and instant-cure properties.

Technology: High-performance aliphatic polyurethane film with nanotechnology adhesive that fills paint imperfections.

Valuable Fact: It has a factory-integrated ceramic top coat, making it extremely hydrophobic without the need for additional ceramic coatings.

Gloss Pro Coverage Levels:
Front Bumper: Protects only the front bumper and headlights. Ideal for preventing damage from road debris.

Front Kit (Partial or Full): * Partial: Bumper, mirrors, and 1/3 of the hood/fenders.

Full: Full hood, full bumper, full front fenders, and mirrors. Eliminates visible lines on the hood.

Full Car: Bumper-to-bumper coverage. Protects every panel against key scratches, parking lot door dings, and vandalism.

3. Matte Paint Protection Film (Satin Effect)
Transforms any glossy paint color into a luxurious matte/satin finish.

Technical Function: Not only protects, but also evenly eliminates the gloss of the original paint.

Advantage: Maintaining a factory-installed matte paint job is extremely difficult (it cannot be polished). This PPF allows you to achieve the matte look with the easy cleaning of a ceramic PPF.

4. Black Paint Protection Film (Deep Black)
Unlike a black vinyl wrap, this is a thick PPF that protects the paint.

Aesthetics: Offers a piano black or matte black finish so deep it looks like real paint, without the "orange peel" texture common in vinyl wraps.

Common Use: Frequently used on roofs to create a "glass roof" effect or to cover the original color on sports cars.

5. Urban Paint Protection Film (Camouflage Style/Pattern)
A line designed for aesthetic customization with protection.

Design: Incorporates patterns (such as carbon fiber, camouflage, or geometric designs) integrated into the film's structure.

Protection: Maintains the same impact resistance properties as Gloss Pro but with a disruptive visual design.

6. Shift Color Paint Protection Film (Color Change)
This is the evolution of the "Wrap." It offers the color change of vinyl but with the durability of armor.

Technology: TPU (thermoplastic polyurethane) film with pigments that change hue depending on the angle of light.

Technical Difference: While colored vinyl can break with a stone, Shift PPF resists impact and self-heals with heat.

Warranty: Generally comes with up to a 12-year warranty, far superior to the 3-5 years of conventional vinyl.

Summary of Technical Specifications (For "Bullet Points"):
Instant Self-Healing: Circular scratches and minor marks disappear without the need for external heat (in the Instant Heal series).

Corrosion Resistance: Protection against bird droppings, tree sap, and acid rain.

Superior Elasticity: Up to 50% more elastic than competitors, allowing for cleaner installation on complex curves.

Nanotechnology Adhesive: Allows the installer to adjust the piece multiple times without leaving glue residue.

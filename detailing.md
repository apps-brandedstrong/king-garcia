1. Car Detailing
The main focus is protection against daily wear and tear and lasting aesthetic enhancement.

Key Technology: Use of nano-ceramic coatings (such as Ceramic Pro 9H and ION) and paint protection films (PPF - KAVACA).

Main Benefits:

Hydrophobic Properties: Repels water, mud, and dirt, facilitating washing (self-cleaning effect).

UV Protection: Prevents oxidation and fading of the paint caused by the sun.

Chemical Resistance: Protects against insect acids, bird droppings, tree sap, and road salt.

9H Hardness: Provides a sacrificial layer that reduces micro-scratches and swirl marks.

Deep Gloss: Intensifies the color and provides a mirror-like finish that lasts for years, not weeks like wax.

Suggested Sections: Exterior Protection, Interior Care (leather, textiles), and Wheel Protection (against brake dust).

2. Motorcycle Detailing
This focuses on protecting exposed surfaces and facilitating maintenance on complex parts.

Key Technology: Coatings that withstand high temperatures and adhere to multiple substrates (metal, chrome, plastic).

Main Benefits:

Heat Resistance: The coatings can be applied to engine parts and exhaust systems to facilitate the cleaning of grease and burnt-on grime.

Component Protection: Protects the fuel tank from light scratches and chemical damage, and protects leather or vinyl seats from UV cracking.

Simplified Washing: Because motorcycles have many hard-to-reach areas, the hydrophobic coating allows dirt to be removed with less mechanical effort.

Versatility: Works on helmets and visors to improve visibility in the rain and prevent insects from sticking.

3. Detailing para Barcos (Boats / Marine)
Aquí el desafío es el ambiente corrosivo del agua salada y la exposición extrema al sol.

Tecnología Clave: Ceramic Pro Marine, diseñado específicamente para gelcoat y superficies marinas.

Beneficios Principales:

Barrera contra el Salitre y Corrosión: Protege el casco y las partes metálicas de la degradación por agua salada.

Protección contra la Oxidación: El gelcoat tiende a volverse "tizoso" (chalky) con el sol; el recubrimiento previene este proceso manteniendo el brillo.

Efecto Anti-incrustante (Fouling Release): Dificulta que las algas, mejillones y la línea de flotación (scum line) se adhieran firmemente, facilitando su eliminación.

Protección de Interiores Marinos: Aplicable en tapicería de vinilo, plásticos y maderas (teca) para evitar manchas de moho, protectores solares y derrames de comida.

Mejora de Eficiencia: Al crear una superficie más lisa y menos porosa, puede reducir la fricción en el agua, mejorando ligeramente la velocidad y el consumo de combustible.
